About Andrew

"Every frame is a decision. Every cut is a heartbeat."

Andrew Sabry is an Asyut-based filmmaker and visual storyteller with over four years of experience crafting cinematic narratives across advertising, weddings, and digital content. His work is defined by an obsessive attention to light, emotion, and rhythm — the invisible architecture that makes a viewer feel something before they understand why.

From high-stakes commercial productions to intimate wedding films, Andrew brings the same cinematic intentionality to every project. He believes that authentic storytelling — rooted in genuine human moments — is the most powerful form of communication.
